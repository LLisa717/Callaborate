How CALlaborate works

Earning and using points

1

Post a Request

Post a request for the task that you need help with under its category. Callaborate will help you to reach out to helpers who have the necessary skills. So sit back and wait for the offers to roll in.

2

Receive Offers

You will receive an offer for every helper that is interested in giving you a hand. You can take the time to review each offering helper's profile, and connect with the helper that best suits your needs.

3

Get Shit Done

Once you confirm an offer, you will be contacted by a helper to coordinate a date and time that works best for the both of you. Get the help you need, and then come back here to review your experience.

Why CALlaborate?

CALlaborate is run for the students, by the students.

Fast, safe services

Everyone you will interact with is just like you, a Berkeley student. No long drives, no bus rides, everyone lives nearby. Close and convenient.

No money required

Get professional advice, academic tutoring, rides from the airport and even more, all without paying a cent! Just pay it forward by helping in whatever way you can.

Reach out to other students, broaden your network and make friends! Grow with Berkeley's sharing economy.
