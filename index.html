How it works

Earning and using points

Earn points

You get 100 points just for signing up! After those first 100, you get new points by contributing to the community. The more you contribute, the more you earn.

Get help

Search on Calaborate to find students offering the services you need. Post what services you need and quickly find someone who can help.

Give back to your community

Reach out to other students, broaden your network and make friends!

Try it now!

Why CALaborate?

CALaborate is run for the students, by the students.

Fast, safe services

Everyone you will interact with is just like you, a Berkeley student. No long drives, no bus rides, everyone lives nearby. Close and convenient.

No money required

Get professional advice, academic tutoring, rides from the airport and even more, all without paying a cent! Just pay it forward by helping in whatever way you can.

Give back to your community

Reach out to other students, broaden your network and make friends! Grow with Berkeley's sharing economy.
